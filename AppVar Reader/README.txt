Welcome to AVReader! This is a simply program that reads AppVars for you if they're not SUPERLocked!

Simply enter in the name of the AppVar (without rowSwap() and it will display the first 9 lines for you!
Use the up and down arrow keys to move through lines. ALPHA will switch between moving 1 line and 9 lines.
Press PRGM to view what line you're at.
Press VARS to view the last line.
Press COS to Goto a line!
DEL, MODE, and CLEAR will exit while ENTER will let you view a different AppVar.

Have fun!
